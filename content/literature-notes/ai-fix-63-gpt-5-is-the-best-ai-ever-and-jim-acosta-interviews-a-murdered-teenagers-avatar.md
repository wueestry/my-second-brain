# GPT-5 IS THE BEST AI EVER, AND JIM ACOSTA INTERVIEWS A MURDERED TEENAGER'S AVATAR | AI FIX #63

---

![[meta/assets/ai-fix.jpg|150]]

## Summary:

The episode “GPT-5 is the best AI ever, and Jim Acosta interviews a murdered teenager's avatar” of the _AI Fix_ podcast delves into two major topics: the release of OpenAI's GPT-5 and the ethical complexities surrounding AI avatars of deceased individuals. The hosts discuss GPT-5’s incremental improvements over previous models, its new features like the real-time router, and the mixed public reception to its launch. They also critically examine a controversial live interview conducted by Jim Acosta with an AI avatar of Joaquin Oliver, a teenager killed in a school shooting, highlighting the ethical dilemmas, privacy concerns, and societal implications of using AI to represent the dead. Throughout, the episode reflects on the broader challenges of balancing AI innovation with responsible and respectful use.

## Key Insights & Analysis:

### GPT-5 as an Incremental but Significant Upgrade

#### Explanation:

GPT-5 is presented as a notable advancement rather than a revolutionary leap towards Artificial General Intelligence (AGI). It features improvements in reducing hallucinations, better instruction following, and less sycophantic behaviour, alongside enhanced creative and reasoning capabilities. The introduction of a "real-time router" that dynamically selects the appropriate model version for a prompt is a key innovation, aiming to optimise user experience despite initial technical hiccups. This insight is significant as it tempers expectations about AI progress while acknowledging meaningful technical refinements.

### Ethical Concerns Around AI Avatars of the Deceased

#### Explanation:

The episode critically explores the use of AI avatars to represent deceased individuals, focusing on the live interview with Joaquin Oliver’s AI avatar. While the parents’ intention was to preserve memory and advocate for gun control, the hosts highlight issues of authenticity, the quality of the AI representation, and the discomfort caused by public use of such avatars. The discussion raises important questions about consent, exploitation, and the blurring of lines between genuine memories and AI-generated impressions, emphasising that such uses should be rare and respectful.

### Privacy and Security Challenges in AI Platforms

#### Explanation:

The podcast addresses a recent privacy issue where a ChatGPT feature unintentionally made some private conversations discoverable via Google search. This raised significant concerns about user data security, especially given the sensitive nature of many conversations. OpenAI’s subsequent removal of this feature reflects the ongoing tension between innovation, user convenience, and privacy protection. This insight is crucial as it highlights the real-world implications of AI deployment on user trust and data security.
