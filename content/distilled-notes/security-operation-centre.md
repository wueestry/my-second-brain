# SECURITY OPERATION CENTRE (SOC)

---

A _Security Operations Centre_ (SOC) is a team of cyber security professionals that monitors the network and its systems to detect malicious cyber security events. Some of the main areas of interest for a SOC are:

- To find **vulnerabilities** in the systems.
- To find **policy violations** in the company
- Determine **unauthorised activities** in the companies systems
- Find possible **network intrusions** and prevent any damage.
