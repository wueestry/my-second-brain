# PHASE MARGIN (PM)

---

Indicates how much more phase shift can be added to the open loop transfer function until the system becomes unstable.

![[meta/assets/gain-phase-margin.png]]

The **Phase Margin (PM)** is the difference between the phase of $G(j\omega)$ and $-180°$ when the magnitude $|KG(j\omega)|$ crosses the unity gain line (0 dB). This point is known as the **gain crossover frequency** ($\omega_c$)

A **positive PM** is generally indicative of a stable system.

The **gain crossover frequency** ($\omega_c$) can also approximate the closed-loop natural frequency, which is related to the step response rise time.
