# MAGNITUDE PLOT

---

The **magnitude plot** (also known as the amplitude curve or gain plot) shows the logarithm of the transfer function's magnitude versus the logarithm of frequency.
The vertical axis for magnitude is typically expressed in decibels ($dB$), where

$$
G_{vdB}(\omega) = 20log_{10}|H(j\omega)| = 20log_{10}\sqrt{Re^2\{H(j\omega)\} + Im^2\{H(j\omega\}j}
$$

This logarithmic scale for magnitude, combined with a logarithmic frequency scale, allows a wide range of frequencies and magnitude changes to be displayed effectively.
