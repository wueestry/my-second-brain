# The Rise of the Ultra Runners A Journey to the Edge of Human Endurance

![cover|150](http://books.google.com/books/content?id=mqOGDwAAQBAJ&printsec=frontcover&img=1&zoom=1&edge=curl&source=gbs_api)

> [!summary]
> The book provides insights into the psychological and social drivers that lead runners to push themselves further, and it explores the allure of ultra running as a way to test human limits.

## Notes

- This book talks about the inspiration of running ultra distances and the challenges you face during such a long race
- A main theme is the idea of quitting, as you always want to end the short term suffering, but the thought that you could have continued haunts you for a long time
