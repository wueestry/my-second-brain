# Transfer Function

> [!abstract]
> A transfer function models a systems output in relationship with its input

$$
\text{Continuous Time:} \qquad H(s) = \frac{Y(s)}{X(s)} = \frac{\mathcal{L}\{y(t)\}}{\mathcal{L}\{x(t)\}}
$$

$$
\text{Discrete Time:} \qquad H(z) = \frac{Y(z)}{X(z)} = \frac{\mathcal{Z}\{y[n]\}}{\mathcal{Z}\{x[n]\}}
$$
